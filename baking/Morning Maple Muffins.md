**Ingredients:**


- 2 cups all-purpose flour

- 1/2 cup packed brown sugar

- 2 teaspoons baking powder

- 1/2 teaspoon salt

- 3/4 cup 2% milk

- 1/2 cup butter, melted

- 1/2 cup maple syrup

- 1/4 cup sour cream

- 1 large egg, room temperature

- 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

*Topping:*

- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour

- 3 tablespoons sugar

- 2 tablespoons chopped nuts 

- 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon

- 2 tablespoons cold butter

**Directions:**


- Preheat oven to 400Â°. In a large bowl, combine flour, brown sugar, baking powder and salt. In another bowl, combine milk, butter, syrup, sour cream, egg and vanilla. Stir into dry ingredients just until moistened.

- Fill greased or paper-lined muffin cups two-thirds full. For topping, combine flour, sugar, nuts and cinnamon; cut in butter until crumbly. Sprinkle over batter.

- Bake 16-20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean. Cool 5 minutes before removing from pans to wire racks. Serve warm.
